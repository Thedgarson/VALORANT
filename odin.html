Odin

"A heavy machine gun that dominates long-range and suppression fire."

In-Game Stats

  • Fire Mode: Fully Automatic
  • Damage:
    • Headshot: 195
    • Body: 65
    • Leg: 49
  • Magazine Capacity: 100
  • Wall Penetration: High
  • Cost: 3200 Credits

Real-World Inspiration

The Odin draws inspiration from the Kord Machine Gun, a Russian heavy machine gun chambered in 12.7×108mm, known for its high rate of fire and effectiveness in suppression roles.

  • Manufacturer: VPK (Military Industrial Company)
  • Caliber: 12.7×108mm
  • Magazine Capacity: 100-150 rounds
  • Effective Range: 2000m
  • Rate of Fire: 700-800 rounds per minute
