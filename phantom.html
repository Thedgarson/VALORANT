Phantom

"A versatile rifle that shines at medium range, excellent for controlled fire."

In-Game Stats

  • Fire Mode: Fully Automatic
  • Damage:
    • Headshot: 195 (0-15m) / 180 (15m+)
    • Body: 39 (0-15m) / 36 (15m+)
    • Leg: 31 (0-15m) / 28 (15m+)
  • Magazine Capacity: 30
  • Wall Penetration: Medium
  • Cost: 2900 Credits

Real-World Inspiration

The Phantom is inspired by the SCAR-H MK17, a battle rifle chambered in 7.62×51mm NATO, known for its high damage and precision at medium to long ranges.

  • Manufacturer: FN Herstal
  • Caliber: 7.62×51mm NATO
  • Magazine Capacity: 20 (standard) or 30 (extended)
  • Effective Range: 500m
  • Rate of Fire: 650 rounds per minute
