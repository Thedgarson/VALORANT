Bucky

"A pump-action shotgun that deals massive damage at close range."

In-Game Stats

  • Fire Mode: Pump-Action
  • Damage:
    • Headshot: 202 (0-15m)
    • Body: 50 (0-15m)
    • Leg: 40 (0-15m)
  • Magazine Capacity: 5
  • Wall Penetration: Low
  • Cost: 900 Credits

Real-World Inspiration

The Bucky is inspired by the Mossberg 500/590, a pump-action shotgun known for its reliability, versatility, and use by both military and law enforcement worldwide.

  • Manufacturer: O.F. Mossberg & Sons
  • Caliber: 12 Gauge
  • Magazine Capacity: 5 (standard) or 7 (extended)
  • Effective Range: 40m (slugs), 20m (buckshot)
  • Rate of Fire: Semi-automatic or Pump-action
