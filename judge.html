Judge

"A rapid-fire shotgun that deals devastating damage up close."

In-Game Stats

  • Fire Mode: Fully Automatic
  • Damage:
    • Headshot: 202 (0-8m) / 160 (8m+)
    • Body: 60 (0-8m) / 48 (8m+)
    • Leg: 45 (0-8m) / 36 (8m+)
  • Magazine Capacity: 7
  • Wall Penetration: Low
  • Cost: 1600 Credits

Real-World Inspiration

The Judge is inspired by the AA-12, an automatic shotgun known for its high rate of fire and extreme stopping power in close-range combat.

  • Manufacturer: Military Armament Corporation
  • Caliber: 12 Gauge
  • Magazine Capacity: 8 (standard) or 20 (extended)
  • Effective Range: 50m
  • Rate of Fire: 300 rounds per minute
